How Textify works

A seamless helper that handles the social friction.

1

Textify Listens

When a call rings, route it to Textify. It answers instantly and live-transcribes the caller's voice directly onto your screen.

2

Tap Your Reply

Read the transcript in real-time. Textify suggests two contextual quick replies, or you can instantly type your own custom response.

3

AI Speaks Naturally

Tap send, and Textify’s ultra-realistic voice engine speaks your reply to the caller. They get an answer, you keep your peace of mind.

You stay completely in control.

Unlike automated AI voice bots that try to hold conversations independently, Textify never decides for you. It acts purely as your voice proxy. You read, you decide, and it executes.
